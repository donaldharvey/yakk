High-quality interviews, like you're in the same room.

A great quality interview relies on great production values. It's easy to record high-quality material when your interviewee's in the same room, but how about online?

We've all heard podcasts where the sound is distant, compressed, and suffers from dropouts. Fireside is your one-stop tool to solve this problem.
